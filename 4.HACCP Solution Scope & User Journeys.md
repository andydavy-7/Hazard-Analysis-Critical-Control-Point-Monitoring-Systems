# HACCP Solution Scope & User Journeys
## Genesis Block: Foundational Narratives Across Three Domains

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** December 2024
**Purpose:** Define solution boundaries, user journeys, critical touchpoints, and generalized narratives for HACCP compliance platform

---

## Executive Summary

This document establishes the **"genesis block"** for the HACCP compliance platform - the foundational scope and narratives that define what we're building and for whom.

### Key Principles:
1. **Domain-Specific Narratives**: Each industry (Food Manufacturing, Pharmaceuticals, Hospitality/Restaurant) has its own generalized narrative
2. **Multi-Perspective Journeys**: Every narrative captures experiences of Compliance Officers, Business Owners, and Floor Staff
3. **Overlapping Foundation**: Despite unique processes, all businesses within a domain share common HACCP compliance challenges
4. **Clear Boundaries**: Explicit IN SCOPE and OUT OF SCOPE definitions prevent feature creep

| **Document Management** | Partner | Store/retrieve HACCP-related documents |

---

### 5.2 The MVP Feature Set (Derived from Narratives)

**Phase 1: Core Platform** (Must-Have for All Domains)

1. **AI HACCP Plan Builder**
   - Industry selection (food, pharma, restaurant)
   - Product/process questionnaire
   - Auto-suggested CCPs and critical limits
   - Template library (50+ industry-specific)
   - Output: Compliant HACCP plan in <1 hour

2. **Mobile Monitoring App**
   - iOS + Android native
   - QR code scanning (equipment, locations)
   - Data entry (temperature, pH, time, etc.)
   - Photo/video capture
   - Offline-first architecture (works without WiFi)
   - Push notifications (task reminders)

3. **Critical Limit Validation**
   - Real-time validation (is reading within limits?)
   - Visual feedback (green/yellow/red)
   - Automatic alerts if out of range

4. **Corrective Action Workflow**
   - Guided wizard (what happened, why, action taken, verification)
   - Photo evidence required
   - Manager approval step
   - Auto-documented audit trail

5. **Audit Report Generation**
   - One-click "Generate Audit Report"
   - Date range selection
   - Export: PDF, Excel
   - Includes: HACCP plan, monitoring logs, corrective actions, signatures

---

**Phase 2: Industry Modules** (Domain-Specific)

**Food Manufacturing Module** (+$50/month):
- FSMA-specific templates
- Allergen management
- Supplier approval workflows
- Lot/batch traceability
- Label compliance checks

**Pharmaceuticals Module** (+$200/month):
- 21 CFR Part 11 compliance (e-signatures, audit trails)
- Pre-validation documentation (IQ/OQ/PQ)
- Batch record integration
- Deviation/CAPA management
- GMP checklists

**Hospitality/Restaurant Module** (+$30/month):
- Multi-location franchise dashboard
- Location rankings and scorecards
- POS integration (Toast, Square)
- Menu-specific hazards
- Shift-based task assignment

---

**Phase 3: Advanced Features** (Differentiation)

- **Plug-and-Play IoT Sensors** ($499 kit + $49/month)
- **Voice-to-text data entry**
- **Multi-language support** (Spanish, Mandarin, Vietnamese)
- **Predictive ML alerts** ("Cooler trending toward failure")
- **API for custom integrations**
- **White-label for consultants**
